teaching

From September 2016 to 2018, I taught at the engineering school of computer sciences and applied mathematics of Grenoble: Ensimag.

I had the full responsibility of a course on computational statistics for 2nd year students (equivalent to fourth year after A-level). I also imparted practical lab work in an advanced course of inferential statistics for 1st year students (equivalent to third year after A-level).

Total teaching time : 128 hours over 2 years.
